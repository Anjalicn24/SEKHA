Popular Question

How do I qualify for a certificate?

To qualify for a genuine Sekha Certificate you must successfully complete your chosen course or Learning Path, achieving 80% or higher in all assessments. Once you complete the final assessment and pass with a result of 80% or more, the course should automatically update to complete. You are now an Sekha Graduate! Well Done!

How can I start a course?

Register or Login to Sekha. Once you login, you can search for a course here. Select the course you want to study and select Join.Y You can then select Start Topic. Alison courses are designed to be self-paced, meaning you can take as much or as little time to complete a course as you wish. Most Certificate courses generally take a learner <2-3 hours to complete and Diploma courses take <8-10 hours to complete.
